Our Departments

Discover the diverse range of departments that make our club thrive.

SOCIAL MEDIA AND MARKETING

Our marketing team ensures strong online presence, running campaigns, and growing our brand on social media platforms.

CONTENT DEPARTMENT

Responsible for creating high-quality, engaging content, from blogs to promotional materials.

MANAGEMENT

Our management team oversees the smooth functioning of all club operations and event execution.

TECHNICAL AND DEVELOPMENT

The tech backbone of our club, focusing on website development, software, and technical support.

OUTREACH

About Proddino

Prodino isn't just a typical club; it's a new thriving community where aspiring innovators come together to brainstorm, collaborate, and bring groundbreaking ideas to fruition.

We Are Proddino

At Prodino, we empower you to turn ideas into products. From prototypes to market-fit solutions, we provide hands-on experience, mentorship, and access to essential resources. Our mission is to cultivate a community of forward thinkers and problem solvers, ready to tackle real-world challenges and make meaningful contributions to society.

Whether your passion lies in technology, design, business, or any field where innovation flourishes, there’s a place for you in Prodino. We encourage diverse perspectives, creativity, and teamwork to push the boundaries of what's possible. Together, we develop innovative solutions, turning visionary concepts into reality.

Recent Events

PRODINNO CURTAIN RAISER
The curtain raiser of our club had a stunning participation of about 400 students. It included guest lectures on product innovation by industry experts, Mr. Gautam Muthuravichandran and Mr. Sriram Balasubramaniam, and an interactive quiz session on product innovation. It was a grand success as the audience got a chance to listen to the insights shared directly by distinguished individuals and engaged with a fun and challenging quiz that tested wits along with knowledge.
POWER BI: From Data to Insights
The event Power BI: From Data to Insights was conducted on a weekend with participation from an engaging audience consisting of day scholars, master's students, and bachelor's students from all years. The event focused on making data-driven business decisions utilizing custom data sets and visualizing them using Power BI. The dedication of the audience to the event drove the enthusiasm of the club members, which made the event worthwhile.
Club Expo
The club expo conducted for freshers for the academic year 2024-25 had engaging activities from the side of Prodinno. Visitors engaged in dares that followed a make-the-throw challenge. The stall, decorated by our zealous members, represented our club motto and the type of events we conduct.
